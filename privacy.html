Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Trello Power-Up: Branch Name.

Data Collection

This Power-Up does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. All data processed is handled locally in your browser via the Trello API.

Data Usage

The Power-Up uses data retrieved from Trello via the API to dynamically generate and copy formatted text (branch name) based on your card and board details. This data is not transmitted or stored outside of your Trello environment.

Third-Party Services

This Power-Up does not use any third-party services or analytics tools. All interactions occur within Trello's environment.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and we encourage you to review this page periodically.
